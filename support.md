# Support

**Last Updated:** December 29, 2025

Welcome to Pano Support! Here you'll find answers to common questions and information about how to use the Pano app.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**What is Pano?**
Pano is a mobile application that allows you to collect, upload, organize, and share files easily. You can create collections, generate shareable links, and collaborate with othersâ€”similar to WeTransfer Collect.

**Is Pano free to use?**
Pano offers free file sharing services. Some features may be subject to usage limits. Check the app for current pricing and plan details.

**Which devices are supported?**
Pano is available for iOS devices. Make sure you're running the latest version of the app for the best experience.

### Account & Sign In

**How do I create an account?**
You can create an account using your email address, or sign in with Apple or Google. Follow the on-screen instructions when you first open the app.

**I forgot my password. What should I do?**
Use the "Forgot Password" option on the sign-in screen. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

**Can I use Pano without an account?**
An account is required to create and manage collections. Sign up to get started.

### File Uploads & Collections

**What types of files can I upload?**
You can upload various file types including images, videos, documents, and more. Check the app for specific file type restrictions and size limits.

**How long are my files stored?**
Files and collections expire automatically after 30 days by default. You can extend this period or delete files manually at any time.

**How do I create a collection?**
Tap the "+" button in the app, select files from your device, and create a new collection. You can name and organize your collection as needed.

**Can I add more files to an existing collection?**
Yes, you can add files to existing collections by opening the collection and using the "Add Files" option.

### Sharing & Links

**How do I share my files?**
Once you've created a collection, tap the "Share" button to generate a shareable link. You can copy the link and send it to others via email, messaging apps, or any method you prefer.

**Who can access my shared links?**
Anyone with the link can access the files in that collection. Keep your links private and only share them with people you trust.

**Can I set a password for my shared links?**
Check the app settings for password protection options. If available, you can add an extra layer of security to your shared collections.

**How do I revoke access to a shared link?**
You can delete the collection or remove individual files from your account. Once deleted, the link will no longer work.

### Privacy & Security

**Is my data secure?**
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) to protect your files both in transit and at rest. Your files are only accessible via the links you generate.

**Do you scan or analyze my files?**
We do not scan or analyze the content of your files unless required for technical reasons (e.g., virus scanning) or legal obligations. Your privacy is important to us.

**Can I delete my account and data?**
Yes, you can delete your account and all associated data from the app settings. Upon deletion, your files will be permanently removed, subject to backup retention policies.

### Troubleshooting

**My upload is failing. What should I do?**

- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you have sufficient storage space
- Verify the file size is within limits
- Try uploading a smaller file first to test
- Restart the app and try again

**I can't access a shared link.**

- Verify the link was copied correctly
- Check if the collection has expired (default: 30 days)
- Confirm the collection hasn't been deleted
- Contact the person who shared the link with you

**The app is crashing or not responding.**

- Close and restart the app
- Update to the latest version from the App Store
- Restart your device
- Check if your device meets the minimum system requirements

**I'm having issues signing in.**

- Verify you're using the correct email and password
- Try resetting your password
- If using Apple or Google sign-in, ensure those accounts are active
- Check your internet connection
